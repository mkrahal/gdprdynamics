Quick Start Guide

GDPR Compliance 360 is preconfigured out-of-the-box with a full array of features to get you up and running in no time. To configure the initial settings navigate to the Setup Wizard and follow these steps:

Configure Data Collection
Select the options that correspond to your data collection needs. (Note: If you are unsure about any of the required information just use the default settings)

Configure Data Sharing
Add shipping companies and other third party entities with which you will be sharing customer data.

Configure Marketing Services
Check the boxes that correspond to the advertizing networks you use to promote your store.

Setup MailChimp Integration (Optional)
From the dropdown list select your email service provider. you will be redirected to your email service provider's website to authenticate and authorize GDPR Compliance 360.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you need assistance you can contact our support team at any time via email at support@gdprdynamics.com or via telephone at +44 (0)20 3289 8606. Please get in touch, we are here to help.

I can't see the GDPR consent notice when I visit my store.
By default, consent notices are designed to only be displayed to vistors who have not yet accepted the GDPR data policy terms. Once a user has accepted the terms, the GDPR data policy notices are no longer displayed. If you have clicked the accept button, you will need to empty your browser's cookies in order to re-display the consent notice.

Furthermore, the GDPR consent notice is configured to only be displayed to European visitors. If you wish to view the GDPR consent notice from outside the European Union you can do so by temporarily deactivating Smart GeoIP Filtering in the Settings tab.

How can I change the default text of my data policy notice in the Privacy Center?
The data policy notice default text can be customized in the Settings tab.
